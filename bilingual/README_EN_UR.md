## English

This folder contains **complete English and Urdu versions** of all NJROS policy and legislative materials prepared for the Government of Pakistan.

| No. | File | Description |
|-----|------|-------------|
| 1 | `NJROS_Cabinet_White_Paper_Bilingual.md` | Cabinet Policy White Paper (v2.0) — **English + Urdu** |
| 2 | `National_Act_Bilingual.md` | National Jirga Registration and Oversight Act, 2026 — **English + Urdu** |
| 3 | `NJROS_Policy_Proposal_Bilingual.md` | Original Policy Proposal (v1.0) — **English + Urdu** |

### Document Hierarchy

1. **Cabinet White Paper** — Primary policy document for Federal Cabinet (recommended)  
2. **Draft Act** — Legislative bill for Parliament  
3. **Policy Proposal** — Detailed foundational policy paper  

### Format

Each bilingual file uses this structure:

```
## Chapter N — ENGLISH
[English text]

## باب N — اردو
[Urdu text]
```

### Note

The Cabinet White Paper (v2.0) reflects revised policy (narrow RCDF scope, NCDOA, no automatic FIR). The Draft Act (v1.0) should be harmonized with the White Paper in a future legislative revision.
